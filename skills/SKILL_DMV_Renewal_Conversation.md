# SKILL.md

## Purpose

This file captures only the knowledge established during this
conversation about preparing for a California DMV driver's license
renewal appointment as an H-1B visa holder.

## DMV Renewal Checklist

### Identity

-   Current California driver's license.
-   Passport.

### Immigration

-   Printed current I-94.
-   Original H-1B approval notice (Form I-797A).

### California Residency

Bring any two acceptable proof-of-residency documents. Examples
discussed: - PG&E bill. - Water utility bill. - AT&T bill.

Notes: - Two documents are sufficient. - Consecutive monthly statements
are not required. - Bank statements are optional if two acceptable
utility bills are already available. - Employment verification is not
required when acceptable residency documents are provided.

### Appointment

-   Bring the appointment confirmation email or at least the
    confirmation code (recommended but typically not strictly required).

### Vision

-   Bring glasses or contact lenses if required for driving.

## Clarifications Learned

### I-94

-   The I-94 is the Arrival/Departure Record documenting current
    immigration status.
-   Printing the current I-94 before the appointment is recommended.
-   Travel history is not required by the DMV.

### H-1B

For an H-1B holder, bringing the passport, I-94, and I-797A helps verify
lawful presence if requested during renewal or REAL ID processing.

## Final Packing List

-   Driver's license
-   Passport
-   Printed I-94
-   Original I-797A
-   Two proof-of-residency documents (e.g., PG&E + Water bill)
-   Appointment confirmation or confirmation code
-   Glasses/contact lenses (if needed)
