# Pokémon GO Skill File

## Core Goal

-   Catch Pokémon.
-   Build strong teams for raids, gyms, and PvP.
-   Collect Pokémon, complete the Pokédex, and gain XP.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Pokémon Quality

## CP

-   Current combat strength.
-   High CP = strong now.
-   CP can be increased by powering up.

## IVs (Potential)

Each Pokémon has three hidden IVs: - Attack (0--15) - Defense (0--15) -
HP (0--15)

Maximum total = 45 (15/15/15).

Powering up and evolving DO NOT change IVs.

## Star Ratings

-   0★ = low IV
-   1★ = low--medium
-   2★ = medium
-   3★ = high
-   4★ = perfect (15/15/15)

Generally: - Keep 3★ and 4★. - Transfer most common 0★--2★ Pokémon.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Investing Stardust

Spend Stardust mainly on: 1. Strong species. 2. High-IV Pokémon. 3.
Lucky Pokémon.

Avoid spending Stardust on every Pokémon.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Transfer vs Trade

## Transfer

-   Permanently deletes Pokémon.
-   Gives Candy.
-   Cannot recover it.

## Trade

-   IVs reroll.
-   Can become better or worse.
-   Lucky trades guarantee minimum 12/12/12 IVs and 50% Stardust
    power-up cost.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Stardust

Best sources: - Catch Pokémon. - Hatch eggs. - Research rewards. - GO
Battle League. - Raids. - Adventure Sync. - Feeding berries at gyms.

Star Piece: - +50% Stardust for 30 minutes.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Research

## Field Research

-   Obtained from spinning PokéStops.
-   Hold up to three tasks.

## Special Research

-   Story quests.
-   Never expire.

## Timed Research

-   Event limited.

Collect 7 daily stamps for a Research Breakthrough.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Friends and Gifts

Benefits: - Items - Stardust - XP - Vivillon progress

Unopened Gifts remain until opened.

Daily Gift opening has a limit.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Postcards

Pinning: - Does NOT consume Item Bag space. - Uses Postcard Book
space. - Default Postcard Book capacity: 350.

You may: 1. Pin postcard. 2. Open Gift. 3. Still receive all items.

Pinning counts immediately for Vivillon.

You can later unpin/delete postcards without losing Vivillon progress.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Quick Catch

There is no option to disable catch animations.

Experienced players use Quick Catch: 1. Hold Berry or Ball menu. 2.
Throw Poké Ball. 3. Release menu. 4. Tap Run. 5. Catch resolves in
background.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Storage Priorities

Keep: - 4★ Pokémon - Strong 3★ Pokémon - Shiny - Legendary - Mythical -
Lucky - Event-exclusive - Best specimen of each useful species

Usually transfer: - Duplicate common 0★--2★ Pokémon.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Beginner Priorities

1.  Reach higher trainer levels.
2.  Catch everything.
3.  Spin PokéStops.
4.  Complete Research.
5.  Add friends.
6.  Save Stardust.
7.  Learn type advantages.
8.  Build strong raid attackers.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Useful Search Filters

-   4\*
-   3\*
-   shiny
-   legendary
-   lucky

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# General Tips

-   Species usually matters more than tiny IV differences.
-   High CP helps immediately.
-   High IV helps long term.
-   Don't invest heavily until you're confident the Pokémon is worth it.
