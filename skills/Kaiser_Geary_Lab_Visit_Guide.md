# Kaiser Geary Lab Visit Guide (Evidence-Based)

## Location

-   Kaiser Permanente San Francisco Medical Center
-   Outpatient Laboratory
-   2238 Geary Blvd, San Francisco, CA

## Hours

-   Monday--Friday: 7:00 AM -- 7:00 PM
-   Saturday--Sunday: 8:00 AM -- 4:00 PM

> Verify hours before visiting, as they may change.

## Best Time to Visit

### Verified personal observations

  Time            Observation                                      Confidence
  --------------- ------------------------------------------------ ----------------
  **\~1:00 PM**   Lab was essentially empty with almost no wait.   High
  **\~4:00 PM**   Lab appeared fairly busy.                        Moderate--High

### Current recommendation

Based on actual personal experience rather than general assumptions:

-   **Preferred arrival time:** around **1:00 PM** for routine blood
    work.
-   There is currently no personal evidence supporting other times of
    day.
-   Continue recording future visits to improve confidence.

### Unknown / Not yet verified

No personal observations yet for: - 7--9 AM - 9--11 AM - 11:30 AM--12:30
PM - 2--3 PM - Weekends

Avoid assuming these periods are busy or quiet until additional
observations are collected.

## STD Screening

### Fasting

-   Routine STI/STD blood and urine screening does **not** require
    fasting.

### Blood tests

Examples: - HIV - Syphilis

Eating beforehand does not affect these tests.

### Urine tests

Examples: - Chlamydia (NAAT) - Gonorrhea (NAAT)

Recommendation: - Avoid urinating for approximately **1 hour** before
providing the urine specimen.

## Before Leaving Home

-   Confirm the clinician has placed the laboratory order.
-   Bring your Kaiser member ID (physical or digital).
-   If providing a urine sample, avoid urinating for about one hour
    before arrival.

## Typical Result Turnaround

-   Chlamydia/Gonorrhea NAAT: approximately 1--3 days
-   HIV and Syphilis blood tests: often 1--3 days

Results usually appear in the Kaiser patient portal.
